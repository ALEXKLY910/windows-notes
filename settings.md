    2. `Notifications`: Turn off notifications
    3. `Power`: Change power profile to "Best performance"
    4. `Advanced->File Explorer`: enable all the dev options
    5. `Advanced`: Enable long paths; Enable sudo(?); Developer Mode (?); 
    6. `Clipboard`: Enable clipboard history

`Bluetooth and Devices`:
    1. Turn off mouse acceleration ("Enhance pointer precision": off)

`Personalization`:
    1. `Taskbar`.
    2. `Lockscreen`.

`Apps`:
    1. `Advanced App settings`: Archive apps off
    2. `Offline Maps`: set update off.
    3. `Resume`: off
    4. `Startup`
    5. `Installed apps`: uninstall all the junk.

`Time & language`:
    1. Set the timezone and time format.

`Accessibility`:
    1. Turn off animation effects and transparency effects

`Privacy & Security`:
